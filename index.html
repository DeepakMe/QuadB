Uttarakhand CM Trivendra Singh Rawat to resign today

Uttarakhand chief minister Trivendra Singh Rawat is likely to resign at a press conference on Tuesday. As per sources, he is scheduled to address a press conference at 3 pm where he will officially announce his exit, said sources. There have been reports of serious discontent from a section of BJP MLAs and MPs, who are bitterly opposed to the chief minister's style of functioning.
